\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{classSystem}{System} responsible for rendering health bars for entities with \doxylink{structLifeComponent}{Life\+Component} and \doxylink{structTagObjectiveComponent}{Tag\+Objective\+Component}. 

This system draws the health bars differently depending on whether the entity has a \doxylink{structTagPlayerComponent}{Tag\+Player\+Component} or not. For player entities, the health bar is drawn at position (100, 50) with a green fill. For other objective entities (e.\+g., obelisks), the health bar is drawn at position (100, 80) with a blue fill.
